Globe Trekkin'

A travel blog
by
Rick Ramble

Week 1: A Tour of London

Highlights: surviving Trafalgar Square, getting kicked out of Buckingham Palace, ringing the doorbell at No. 10 Downing Street

After landing at Heathrow, I rode the Tube to my hotel, dropped off my bag, and hit the ground running. First stop: The Natural History Museum, where I checked out all the dinosaur skeletons and took a small souvenir for my dog Marley back in Seattle. For lunch, I grabbed some bangers and mash at a pub called The Jolly Slophouse, then went on a guided tour of the Tower of London. Fed the pigeons in Hyde Park. Took in a Shakespeare play at the Globe theater but didn't understand a word of it. I think someone said "Forsooth".

Week 2: Seeing the Sights of Munich

Highlights: Dinner at the Hofbräuhaus, sleeping through the Bavarian State Opera, attending bratwurst-eating contests

My Octoberfest activities list:

  1. Drink beer
  2. Dance the polka
  3. Sing songs I can't pronounce with everyone
  4. Eat giant pretzels
  5. Drink more beer

Week 3: Wandering through Paris

Highlights: Getting lost in les Catacombes, putting lipstick on the Mona Lisa, dropping water balloons from the Eiffel Tower

What a busy week it was! I toured the Louvre. I jogged the Champs-Elysées from the Arc de Triomphe to the Tuileries Garden and ran over 5 poodles. I cruised the Seine River and was attacked by a swan. I went to a flea market and bought a chamberpot used by Napoleon Bonaparte (or so I was told). I ate lots of baguettes, crepes, truffles, and creme brulee. I learned how to say Alka-Seltzer in French.

Stayed tuned for my adventures in Moldova!
