The Music Scene

Seattle Symphony to Perform Beethoven Backwards

For a change of pace, Seattle Symphony conductor Ludovic Morlot has opted to
play the entire Beethoven's seventh symphony backwards. "Not to worry",
he commmented, "It'll sound exactly like something by Shostakovich."

Local Band Fleet Foxes to Change Name to Furry Faces

"Everyone wears massive facial hair, that's our image", said lead singer Robin Pecknold.
"Sure, we'll let girls in the band, but they'll have to put on fake beards."
